Who It's For

Buyers who need sustained high-capacity resawing that 1 and 1.75 HP machines cannot provide. The 3 HP motor at 230V handles full-width resawing in dense hardwood without strain, and the 14-inch resaw capacity covers the most demanding hobbyist and semi-professional work. Requires a dedicated 230V circuit and substantial floor space. Buyers who do not regularly push resawing limits should evaluate whether this investment is warranted compared to a 1.75 HP machine at roughly half the cost.

What Owners Praise

  • Three HP motor handles demanding hardwood resawing without strain - community consistently notes it cuts anything like butter
  • Excellent fit and finish with precision-ground cast iron surfaces
  • All adjustments are tool-free including tracking, guides, and tension
  • Dust collection performance is notably better than expected for a bandsaw
  • Welded steel frame at nearly 400 lbs dampens vibration to a level other saws cannot match

What Owners Report as Limitations

  • Requires 230V dedicated circuit restricting placement and adding electrical cost
  • At nearly 400 lbs positioning requires a hand truck and a second person
  • Dust collection screen clogs with green wood shavings and thin strips during extended resawing
  • Price is significantly above 1.75 HP competitors that cover most hobbyist resawing needs
  • 80-inch overall height can be a problem for low-clearance shop spaces

Upgrades & Compatibility

Accepts 153-inch blades 1/8 to 1 inch wide. Four-inch dust port. All adjustments tool-free. Includes rip fence and miter gauge. Large 21.5x16 cast iron table. Powermatic stocks OEM replacement blades (PM1500-021). Compatible with Woodslicer, Lenox, and Timber Wolf blades. 230V single-phase prewired. Mobile base sold separately.

Alternatives to Consider

  • Laguna 1412 — The Laguna 14|12 offers 1.75 HP with ceramic guides on 110V at roughly half the price for buyers who do not need 3 HP performance.
  • Jet Jwbs 14dxpro — The Jet JWBS-14DXPRO covers most serious hobbyist resawing needs at a fraction of the price with 1.25 HP and 12-inch capacity.
