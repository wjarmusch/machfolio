Who It's For

Buyers who want a compact cast iron benchtop bandsaw for curved cuts, scrollwork, and light woodturning blank preparation. The 1/3 HP induction motor is quiet and reliable for softwoods and light hardwoods. Buyers expecting to resaw hardwood thicker than 3 inches regularly or who need production-level output should look at a 14-inch floor model instead.

What Owners Praise

  • Cast iron table is large and flat for its class
  • Induction motor runs quietly with minimal vibration
  • Lightweight at 68 lbs makes repositioning practical
  • Fence system provides repeatable rip cuts for hobby work
  • Widely discussed with substantial community owner data

What Owners Report as Limitations

  • 4-5/8 inch resaw capacity limits the saw to thin stock
  • Fence quality is criticized in community as imprecise
  • Blade guide bearings can shift during tightening requiring careful readjustment
  • No worklight included
  • Table vibrates noticeably during cuts compared to heavier floor models

Upgrades & Compatibility

Accepts 70-1/2 inch blades 1/8 to 1/2 inch wide. Rikon sells a Deluxe Rip Fence upgrade (10-900) that addresses the stock fence complaints. Compatible with aftermarket blade guides from Iturra Design and Carter. Standard 2-1/2 inch dust port. Miter gauge included.
